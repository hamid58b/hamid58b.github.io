The Problem: A Data Bottleneck

Life sciences are drowning in data. Researchers spend 50-80% of their time just cleaning redundant, error-prone public databases. This siloed, manual process slows discovery and delays critical breakthroughs.

Redundant Data

Public databases are filled with errors and duplications, making reliable analysis difficult.

Siloed Pipelines

Data cleaning pipelines are isolated, hindering AI/ML adoption and reproducibility.

Slower Discovery

The result is missed biological insights and a slower pace of innovation.

The A10i Solution

Our platform ingests messy, siloed data from any source and uses a suite of AI components to deliver clean, analysis-ready datasets on demand.
